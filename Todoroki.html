Todoroki

Quirk: Half-Cold Half-Hot

Todorki is son of one of the second best hero, Endeavor. He has a very complicated relationship with his dad. His hatred towards his dad is what motivates him to do his best. He gets fire from his dad and ice from his mom. He refuses to use the side of fire. He was one of the recommended into U.A which is the top hero academy in the world.
